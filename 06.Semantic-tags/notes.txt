<header>, <footer>, <main>, <nav>, <article>, <section>:


<header>: Typically contains introductory content or navigational aids for its parent section or document.
<footer>: Holds footer content, often containing copyright, contact information, or related links.
<main>: Encloses the primary content of the document, excluding headers, footers, and navigation links.
<nav>: Marks a section containing navigation links or menus.
<article>: Represents an independent, self-contained piece of content, such as a blog post, article, or comment.
<section>: Defines a thematic grouping within a document; it's a generic container when there isn't a more specific semantic element available.
